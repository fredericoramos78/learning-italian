## Plural

|Before ...| Masculine | Feminine|
|---|---|---|
|consonant| i | le |
|vowel| gli | le |
|`-z`, `-y`, or `-s` + consonant|gli|le|

* Feminine is always `le``
* Masculine is :
  * `i` for consonant, with exceptions
  * `gli` for everything else (including the consonant exceptions)
