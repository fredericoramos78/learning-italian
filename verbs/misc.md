# Other verbs

## `-are` verbs
* abitare - to live
* amare - to love
* arrivare - to arrive
* ascoltare - to listen to
* ballare - to dance
* cantare - to sing
* chiamare - to call
* comprare - to buy
* costare - to cost
* invitare - to invite
* lavorare - to work
* luccicare - to sparkle
* mangiare - to lunch
* pranzare - to have lunch
* prenotare - to book
* proviare - to try / taste

## `-ere` verbs

* conoscere - to know / to meet
* leggere - to read
* ricevere - to receive
* sapere - to know
* scrivere - to write
* spendere - to spend

* morire - to die
* offrire - to offer
